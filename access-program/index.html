Advanced Infrastructure

Available Lab Equipment & Facilities

Our access program connects members with cutting-edge tools across specialized micro-labs, managed by physical prototyping engineers.

3D Printing & Fab Lab

High-speed prototyping utilizing Bambu Lab FDM platforms and SLA resin printers. Support for PLA, PETG, ABS, and TPU materials.

Electronics & IoT Lab

Equipped with Rigol Digital Oscilloscopes, waveform generators, multi-channel power supplies, logic analyzers, and premium hot-air soldering stations.

Wood & Metal Workshop

Direct access to scroll saws, precise drill presses, high-RPM rotary tools, sanding stations, and heavy-duty hand tools.

PCB Fabrication Lab

Custom PCB milling machines for rapid double-sided board extraction, chemical etching setups, and SMD component pick-and-place stations.

Laser Cutting Studio

40W CO2 Laser cutter and engraver. Precision cutting and marking of acrylic, wood, leather, and sheet materials for custom casings.

Physical AI Arena

Dedicated test environments, visual tracking arrays, obstacle tracks, and reference setups for mobile robots (OpenDuck) and humanoid arms (Reachy Mini).
