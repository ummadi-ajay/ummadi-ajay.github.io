Our programs are designed for different age groups and learning stages:

Beginner Program, ages 8 to 10
A creative introduction to robotics, coding, electronics, sensors, circuits, 3D design, and hands-on project building.

Intermediate Program, ages 11 to 14
A deeper engineering track covering Arduino programming, electronics, IoT, robotics control, computer vision basics, AI/ML fundamentals, and real-world embedded projects.

Advanced Program, ages 15+
A serious technical pathway for students ready to explore ROS 2, autonomous robotics, machine learning, edge AI, advanced prototyping, and portfolio-worthy engineering projects.

Students at MakerWorks learn through:

- Hands-on projects instead of only theory
- Robotics, coding, electronics, AI, IoT, and 3D design
- Small-group mentorship from experienced makers and engineers
- Competition preparation for platforms such as WRO, VEX IQ Robotics, Coolest Projects, and Mekathlon
- A structured long-term curriculum that helps students grow steadily and confidently
