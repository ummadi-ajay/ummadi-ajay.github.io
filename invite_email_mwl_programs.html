Choose the right program for your child

Beginner Program
Ages 8 to 10
A creative introduction to robotics, coding, electronics, sensors, circuits, 3D design, and hands-on project building.
Intermediate Program
Ages 11 to 14
A deeper engineering track covering Arduino programming, electronics, IoT, robotics control, computer vision basics, AI/ML fundamentals, and embedded projects.
Portfolio Building Program
Ages 15+ | Selective university readiness
A serious technical pathway for ROS 2, autonomous robotics, machine learning, edge AI, advanced prototyping, and documented engineering projects. Strong portfolios help selective universities, including Ivy League schools, see initiative, depth, and real problem-solving beyond grades.
