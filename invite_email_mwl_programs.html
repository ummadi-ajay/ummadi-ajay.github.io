Choose the right program for your child

Beginner Program
Ages 8 to 10
A creative introduction to robotics, coding, electronics, sensors, circuits, 3D design, and hands-on project building.
Intermediate Program
Ages 11 to 14
A deeper engineering track covering Arduino programming, electronics, IoT, robotics control, computer vision basics, AI/ML fundamentals, and embedded projects.
Advanced Program
Ages 15+
A serious technical pathway for ROS 2, autonomous robotics, machine learning, edge AI, advanced prototyping, and portfolio-worthy engineering projects.
