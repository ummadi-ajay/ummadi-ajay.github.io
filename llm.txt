# MakerWorks - Robotics & Coding Lab for Kids in Mumbai

MakerWorks is a premier maker space and robotics academy located in Malad East, Mumbai. We specialize in hands-on STEM education for children and teens aged 8 to 17.

## Educational Programs
- **Beginner Level (Ages 7-10):** A 2-year program focusing on robotics, coding, and electronics using micro:bit and creative projects.
- **Intermediate Level (Ages 11-14):** A 2-year technical program to master Arduino fundamentals, electronics basics, and 3D industrial design.
- **Advanced Level (Ages 15+):** Elite 2-year technical program mastering Industrial Robotics (ROS 2), Machine Learning, and Professional CAD design.

## Technical Expertise
Our students and mentors work with:
- **Hardware:** Arduino, Raspberry Pi, ESP32, Sensors, Motors, and Actuators.
- **Software:** Python, C++, Visual Coding, ROS 2 (Robot Operating System).
- **Emerging Tech:** Artificial Intelligence (AI), Internet of Things (IoT), Industry 4.0.
- **Design:** 3D Printing, Professional CAD, CNC, and Prototyping.

## Major Competitions
MakerWorks facilitates student participation in:
- **World Robot Olympiad (WRO):** Creative robotics and problem-solving.
- **VEX Robotics:** Design, build, and drive in global STEM challenges.
- **Coolest Projects:** Showcasing student innovations and digital creativity.
- **Mekathalon:** Premier showcase for mechanical engineering and sustainable robotics.

## Learning Philosophy
MakerWorks follows a "Learn by Doing" approach. We provide a collaborative environment where students move from being consumers of technology to creators, building real-world engineering solutions.
