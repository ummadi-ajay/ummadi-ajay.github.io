# MakerWorks Lab Hardware Partnership Brief

## Lab

MakerWorks Lab is a robotics and maker space in Malad East, Mumbai. Students build practical projects across robotics, electronics, Arduino, Raspberry Pi, Python, AI, IoT, computer vision, and 3D design.

We work mostly with open-source hardware, open robotics tools, and maker-friendly ecosystems. We like hardware that students can learn from, modify, document, and use in real projects rather than only consume as a black box.

## Students and programs

- Age group: 8 to 17
- Current student count: 30+ students, based on existing site copy. Update before sending.
- Learning model: hands-on builds, small-group mentorship, project documentation, competition preparation.
- Competition tracks: WRO, VEX IQ Robotics, Coolest Projects, Mekathlon.

## Why partner with us

MakerWorks can act as a South Asia education and robotics testbed. We can put new hardware into real student projects quickly, document what works, and share structured feedback from mentors and learners.

When a product performs well, we can also recommend it through MakerWorks official channels, use it in student projects, and explore deeper collaboration with the vendor's education, DevRel, or product teams.

## Hardware areas we want to evaluate

- Edge AI and robotics compute: Jetson, Raspberry Pi, Arduino Pro, ESP32, AI accelerators.
- Sensors: LiDAR, depth cameras, IMUs, GNSS, encoders, ToF, thermal sensors.
- Motion: smart servos, BLDC drivers, motor controllers, gear motors, mecanum systems.
- Build systems: robotics channels, bearings, fasteners, chassis, FTC/VEX-style parts.
- 3D printing: printers, filament, spare parts, high-temperature materials.
- Embedded and semiconductor kits: evaluation boards, sensor shields, motor-control boards.
